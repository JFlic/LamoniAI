# Prestigious Honors Day

You’re the kind of creative, motivated, high-achieving student that Graceland University is looking for.

Hosted by the Honors Program, Prestigious Honors Day is an opportunity for high school seniors and transfer students with a 3.25 or higher GPA to be rewarded for their academic achievement.

Only students who participate in Prestigious Honors Day have the opportunity to receive the coveted Prestigious Honors Scholarship – up to a full-tuition award that is renewable for up to four years. To be interviewed for the Prestigious Honors Scholarship, students must have a 3.50+ gpa.

Students with a 3.25+ GPA are invited to attend Prestigious Honors Day and be interviewed for an Honors Scholarship and admission into the Honors Program. Students who are admitted and participate in the Honors Program will receive a variable amount each year renewable for up to four years. Event better, the Honors Scholarship can be awarded in addition to your primary scholarship (can not be combined with a full-tuition award such as the Prestigious Honors Scholarship).

The day begins at 9:30 a.m.

PRESTIGIOUS HONORS SCHOLARSHIP –full tuition per academic year, renewable for four years

One of Graceland’s most sought after scholarships, the Prestigious Honors Scholarship is awarded annually to three students (one Prestigious Honors Scholarship is awarded at each Prestigious Honors Day). Each scholarship competitor will go through an interview process as part of their eligibility.

HONORS PROGRAM Scholarship– $1000 per academic year for up to four years.

The Honors Scholarship is awarded to all students who apply and are selected to be members of the Graceland Honors Program. Students wishing to join the Honors Program will go through an interview process as part of the application process. Students are expected to actively participate in the Honors Program in order to continue receiving the Honors Program Scholarship.
