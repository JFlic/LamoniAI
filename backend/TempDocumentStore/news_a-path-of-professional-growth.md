# A Path of Professional Growth | Graceland

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/news/a-path-of-professional-growth/#top

Recent School of Nursing graduate Lindsey Davis is featured in the National Student Nurses’ Association membership magazine

February 8, 2024 | jns

With incredible passion and commitment,Graceland University School of Nursingstudents shine in nursing excellence long before they graduate. They attend conferences and create connections across the nation, join professional associations, and chronicallyimpress award and honors committeeswith their leadership skills in the field.

One such honoree is Lindsey Davis ’23, a December graduate who was recognized as a future nurse leader by the Leadership University Honor Society of the National Student Nurses’ Association (NSNA).

The freshly published issue of the NSNA membership magazineImprintfeatures a portrait of Lindsey’s personal ambition and professional growth. Head right over to the online edition to read about the multifaceted roles she takes on as asA trailblazing force in education & healthcare.[→Up Closesection, pages 9-10]

***TheNational Student Nurses’ Association (NSNA)is dedicated to fostering the professional development of nursing students. With 50,000 members nationwide, NSNA is the voice of the future of ​the nursing profession.
