# Graceland University Alumni Benefits and Advantages | Graceland University

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/alumni/benefits-advantages/#top

Students today. Alumni forever.

We want to provide alumni the services they need for the rest of their lives. Whether it’s finding employment, registering for a SkillPath seminar or getting a transcript, we want to make it easy for you to get the information you need.

JUMP TO

Show your Graceland pride with a new Graceland sweatshirt, hat or cup. Visit the Spirit Shop in the Newcom Student Union. Our online store has exclusive items, so be sure to check it out!

For recent graduates and alumni who may be changing careers or entering graduate school, choose the links to the Registrar’s Office to request a transcript.

Available to students who will be attending Graceland University full-time on the Lamoni, IA campus who are direct descendants of Graceland alumni. The Alumni Endowed Scholarship is for variable amounts and once awarded is renewable for up to four years.

Graceland offers a variety of services to its alumni.

Graceland's alumni magazine is full of stories about Graceland alums, current students, faculty, and more. You can view archived issues on our website.

Graceland alumni may register for one free SkillPath or National Seminars training session per year. Contact Alumni Programs with the date, location and name of the seminar you would like to attend. Call toll-free 866.GU4.EVER or contact us below.

### Brick Plaza

Make a path to your legacy by purchasing a brick at in the Alumni Brick Plaza. Proceeds benefit the Alumni Endowed Scholarship Fund, which exists for the sole purpose of providing financial assistance to descendants of Graceland alumni.
