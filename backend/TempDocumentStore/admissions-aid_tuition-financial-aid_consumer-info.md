# Consumer Info

We want to help you make the best decisions possible.

We have assembled these informational resources to enable students and their families to make informed decisions regarding their education. Most of this information is also available in theonline catalog.

If you have questions or need a paper copy of any information, please contact Graceland University Student Financial Services atmyfinancials@graceland.edu.
