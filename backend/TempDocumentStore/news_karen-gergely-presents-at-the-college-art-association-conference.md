# Gergely Presents at College Art Conf. | Graceland

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/news/karen-gergely-presents-at-the-college-art-association-conference/#top

March 11, 2021

Karen Gergely, MFA, Associate Professor of Art and colleague Ellen Muller presented a live edition of the CAA Conversations Podcast: Sustainable Design: Beyond the Stuff, Towards the System: A conversation between Rachel Beth Egenhoefer and Peter Dean, at the College Art Association Conference Feb 10-13. The conversation addressed the conference theme of Sustainability.

This podcast is part of a larger series of podcasts for the national organization that Gergely has been managing for the past two years. Currently in its fourth season, CAA Conversations primarily focus on pedagogy, diversity, and inclusion in the university setting and can be found on the CAA website, iTunes and SoundCloud.
