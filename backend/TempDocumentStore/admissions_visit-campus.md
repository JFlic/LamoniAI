# Visit Campus - Graceland University in IA or MO

Source URL: http://www.graceland.edu/admissions/visit-campus#top

Save Your Spot for Fall 2025 on the Lamoni Campus

Visiting campus either in person or virtually is one of the best ways to see just how special Graceland is. Plus, we’d love to meet you!

The best way to learn about life as a Yellowjacket is to experience it for yourself! We invite you to visit our beautiful campuses in Lamoni, IA and Independence, MO to see what possibilities await you at Graceland University.
