# Vanessa K. King, DC, MS, MTAA

Faculty Associate in the Nutrition & Human Performance MS Program

DC, Chiropractic, Logan UniversityMS, Nutrition and Human Performance, Logan UniversityBS, Human Biology, Logan University

vkk1@graceland.edu

Vanessa K. King, DC, MS, MTAA is a graduate of Logan University having obtained a Doctorate in Chiropractic followed by a Master of Science Degree in Nutrition and Human Performance with highest honors. She then went on to work in private practice and taught functional nutrition courses as a Faculty Instructor at Logan University. Currently, Dr. King is privileged to be working with Graceland University in the Division of Nutrition and Human Performance.

Learning, teaching, and bringing functional nutrition into practical application is her passion. Dr. King enjoys the collaboration of working with the next generation of individuals influencing health. She believes strongly that exploring the intricate interconnections of nutrition and how broadly it affects overall health can change the world, even if it’s through one person, or one family at a time.
