The Alumni Endowed Scholarship (AES) fund provides financial assistance to direct descendants of Graceland University alumni.

Applicant must:

Relatives of selection committee members are ineligible to receive an Alumni Endowed Scholarship award.

If you have any questions, please contact the Alumni Programs Office at 866.484.3837 (toll-free in the US and Canada) oralumni@graceland.edu.

Anyone who completes 12 semester hours of course work at Graceland is considered an alumnus. Each year, the Graceland Alumni Board awards a limited number of scholarships. Applications for this scholarship are accepted starting on January 1 with a final submission deadline of March 1 for the following fall semester. The Alumni Board Fundraising & Awards Committee determines each year’s recipients. Once awarded, the AES is renewable for up to four years.

*If you do not have access to the appropriate technology to complete the application online, please contact the Alumni Programs Office and an application packet that you can complete manually and submit by mail will be provided to you.
