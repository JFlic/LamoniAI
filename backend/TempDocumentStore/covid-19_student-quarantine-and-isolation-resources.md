# Student Isolation and Quarantine Resources | COVID-19 Updates

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/covid-19/student-quarantine-and-isolation-resources/#top

Have you been directed to quarantine or isolate? The information below will help.

You have had direct exposure to someone who tested positive for COVID-19.

Asymptomatic Quarantine:If you are required to quarantine but are asymptomatic,you will remain in quarantine for a minimum of seven days.If you test negative after seven days, you may leave quarantine. If you choose not to be tested, you must remain in quarantine for an additional three days – 10 days total.

Symptomatic Quarantine:If you are required to quarantine and are experiencing symptoms,you will remain in quarantine for 14 days.

If at any time while in quarantine you test positive, you will be moved to isolation.

If you want to leave quarantine housingbefore you are released from quarantine by the COVID coordinator, you must alert Residence Life (641.784.5107) and let them know of your planned departure. Note that you may NOT enter the residence halls or other campus buildings and may only return with the approval of the COVID coordinator.

You have tested positive for COVID-19.Isolation lasts for at least 5 days. Before release, you must be symptom-free for 24 hours. Following release, you are expected to wear a mask at all times for the next 5 days.

If you want to leave isolation housingbefore you are released from isolation by the COVID coordinator, you must alert Residence Life (641.784.5107) and let them know of your planned departure. Note that you may NOT enter the residence halls or other campus buildings and may only return with the approval of the COVID coordinator.
