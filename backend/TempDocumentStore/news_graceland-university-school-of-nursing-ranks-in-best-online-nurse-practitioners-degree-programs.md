# GU Nursing Among Best Online NP Programs | Graceland

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/news/graceland-university-school-of-nursing-ranks-in-best-online-nurse-practitioners-degree-programs/#top

August 1, 2017

Graceland University School of Nursing has ranked twice in the 25 Best Online Nurse Practitioners Degree Programs for 2017 by College Choice.

The rankings included the top 15 MSN-NP (Master of Science in Nursing to Nurse Practitioner) programs and the top 10 DNP (Doctor of Nursing Practice) programs. Graceland ranked in each category: eleventh in the top MSN-NP programs and tenth in the top DNP programs.

College Choice determined selected programs taking into account such factors as reputation, graduation rates, selectivity and faculty resources. Find the full listhere.

According to their website, College Choiceoffers numerous free online resources to help students in their college search, including unique, student-centered college rankings, admissions advice, scholarship and financial aid information, and much more. Learn more about College Choicehere.

Learn more about Graceland University School of Nursing – both online and in-person programs –here.
