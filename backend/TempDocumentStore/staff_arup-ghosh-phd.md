# Arup Ghosh, PhD

Department Chair Math and Computer Science, Assistant Professor of Computer Science & Information Technology (CSIT)

PhD: Industrial Engineering, Ajou University, Suwon, South KoreaMS: Computer Science, University of Trento, Trento, ItalyBT: Computer Science & Engineering, WBUT University, Kolkata, India

aghosh1@graceland.edu

Cell:641.572.9171

Resch Science and Technology Hall, Rm# 119

Dr. Ghosh is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Graceland University. Born in Barasat, India, in 1989, he pursued his higher education internationally, obtaining a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Trento, Italy, and a PhD in Industrial Engineering from Ajou University, South Korea. Before joining Graceland, Dr. Ghosh was a faculty member at Vellore Institute of Technology in India and worked as a Research and Development Engineer at UDMTEK Corporation in South Korea. His academic expertise covers a broad range of computer science disciplines, with a particular focus on teaching courses in Machine Learning, Web Programming, Artificial Intelligence, and Computer Networking.
