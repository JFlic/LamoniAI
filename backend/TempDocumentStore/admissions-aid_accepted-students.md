Congratulations! We’re glad to have you. Here’s what you do next.

Now that you’ve been accepted as an official future Gracelander, there are a few things you need to do to secure your place and get ready for your new adventure on theLamoni campus. Use this checklist to make sure you’re taking all the right steps and always feel free to reach out to us with any questions.

Contact your admissions counselor to discuss your next steps – and welcome to the start of a great adventure!
