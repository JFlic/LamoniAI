# Career Opportunities | Graceland University

Source URL: http://www.graceland.edu/employment#top

Come join our community and help change the world with us.

Graceland University creates a community for all employees to grow and learn. The university invites you to apply for any of our open positions on our campuses in Lamoni, Iowa or Independence, Missouri. We provide acompetitive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance. Updates are made regularly, so please check back often. Graceland offers a generous paid time off policy, which includes vacation, sick leave and holiday pay.

Founded in 1895 and sponsored byCommunity of Christ, Graceland University is much more than a school. Graceland is a community of passionate, caring and dedicated individuals who make up a worldwide community network that reaches far beyond its two campuses.

Graceland University welcomes your application and interest in the open positions listed on this website. Updates are made regularly, so please check back often.

All applications/resumes are kept on file for one year. If you see future positions that interest you, please let us know so we may submit your application materials. Reference letters will not be accessible to applicants during the application process nor after becoming an employee.

Explore Graceland's three campuses where students can learn.

Our Lamoni campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities such as the renovated $16 million Shaw Center, the Helene Center for the Visual Arts, Resch Science and Technology Hall, and the Fitzgerald Fitness Center.

Graceland’s Independence, Missouri, campus in the metropolitan Kansas City area hosts our School of Nursing and the Community of Christ Seminary as well as other in-person and online undergraduate and graduate programs.

Graceland offers online programs in many areas for students seeking to advance their career or just needing the flexibility of a working schedule.
