# Lamoni, IA Campus | Graceland University

Source URL: http://graceland.edu/about/campus-locations/lamoni-campus/#top

Discover quality undergraduate and graduate programs at our main, residential campus within walking distance of Lamoni, Iowa.

What started in 1895 with just 18 students on the second floor of a building in downtown Lamoni, Iowa has now become a four-year accredited university, offering 35+ academic programs to over 1,400 students annually.

Home to just under 800 enrolled students, our beautiful and welcoming main, residential campus of Graceland University is always buzzing with activity. Located in Lamoni, Iowa, Graceland offers the safety, security and tranquility of small town life while still providing plenty of activities for our students to enjoy.

Higher education is still one of the best investments you can make in your future: higher income potential, greater career advancement, a strong, lifelong network, and the self-confidence to pursue your passion are all part of the Graceland Experience.

With over 30 undergraduate majors and minors, at Graceland you’re sure to find an area of study you’ll not only love but that you can turn into a lifetime career.

At Graceland, there’s always something fun to do. With 22 varsity athletic teams and 40 clubs and organizations, you’ll have plenty of activities to choose from.

Located just a quick walk away from campus, students can easily take advantage of everything this small town has to offer.

Grab a drink at the local coffee house.
Catch a movie at the Coliseum Theatre.
Explore the town from the local bike trail.
Head to one of the parks to play games or relax.
Pick up some veggies at the farmer’s market.

Small-town life is relaxing, but sometimes you need a change of pace. With Des Moines just over an hour away, Kansas City less than two and Omaha just under three, there are still plenty of opportunities to enjoy the big city every now and again.

The best way to learn what Graceland is all about is to see it for yourself! Located just over an hour south of Des Moines, Iowa, our main, residential campus in Lamoni is situated on 170 acres of beautiful rolling hills just waiting to be explored.

Getting to the Lamoni, Iowa Campus is easy.

Driving from the SouthTravel I-35 North to Exit 4 (first exit in Iowa). Turn left off of the exit onto US 69, and drive about 4 to 5 miles. Turn left on Cherry Street. Turn left onto College Avenue, and follow it to the four-way stop. Turn right, and follow the road to the main parking lot. Please drive onto Circle Drive, and park in a reserved space for visitors.

Driving from the NorthTravel I-35 South to Exit 4 (last exit in Iowa). Turn right off of the exit onto US 69, and drive about 4 to 5 miles. Turn left on Cherry Street. Turn left onto College Avenue, and follow it to the four-way stop. Turn right, and follow the road to the main parking lot. Please drive onto Circle Drive, and park in a reserved space for visitors.

Graceland Airport Shuttle Serviceoperates at the beginning and ending of the fall and spring semesters as well as at the beginning and ending of Spring Break. The shuttle services has service to/from the Kansas City International Airport and Des Moines International Airport.Visit the Shuttle Service pageto find a schedule, fees and other restrictions. Space is limited so schedule early.

Legacy Charters(not affiliated with Graceland) provides a bus to students traveling between Graceland University and the Kansas City International Airport for a fee. Please contact Legacy Charters for more information.

*Mileage will vary based on route and reference point.
