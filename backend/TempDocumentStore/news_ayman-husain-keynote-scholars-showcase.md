# Ayman Husain ’95 To Keynote Scholars’ Showcase | Graceland

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/news/ayman-husain-keynote-scholars-showcase/#top

The annual Scholars’ Showcase event will be held April 11, 2024

March 26, 2024

Graceland University will hold its annualScholars’ ShowcaseApril 11, 2024, where alumnus Ayman Husain will be this year’s Keynote Speaker. Husain graduated from Graceland University with his Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering in 1995.

Husain is a seasoned data and AI leader who has been instrumental in driving the adoption of cutting-edge generative AI solutions. With a deep understanding of the transformative power of AI, Husain has been a thought leader in Microsoft’s AI initiatives.

Husain’s expertise spans various industries, from technical intricacies to strategic implementations to help all achieve more with less. He has a proven track record of helping businesses unlock their potential through the power of AI, thereby contributing to the mission of empowering every person and organization on the planet to achieve more.

He firmly believes in the transformative role of education in shaping the future of AI and is committed to fostering a culture of innovation and curiosity among the learners.

“Ayman’s expertise in generative artificial intelligence and how it is employed in business is a crucial message for Graceland University students to hear. AI is transforming the cultural and business landscape, and Graceland students need to be prepared for this new reality,” saidJoel Shrock, PhD, Vice President for Academic Affairs and Dean of the Faculty at Graceland.
