# School of Nursing Ranked Second in Iowa for Nurse Practitioners | Graceland

Source URL: https://www.graceland.edu/news/school-of-nursing-ranked-second-in-iowa-for-nurse-practitioners/#top

Graceland’s heralded School of Nursing continues to be a leader in nurse education

October 25, 2024

Graceland University is proud to announce that its School of Nursing has beenranked the #2 nurse practitioner program in the state of Iowa by RegisteredNursing.org. This prestigious recognition reflects the school’s dedication to excellence in education, clinical practice, and preparing future leaders in healthcare.

The ranking places Graceland’s program among the top choices for aspiring nurse practitioners, underscoring the university’s commitment to high-quality instruction and hands-on learning.

Dr. Sharon Little-Stoetzel, Dean of theSchool of Nursing, expressed her pride in the accomplishment, saying, “This recognition is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our faculty, staff, and students. We focus on preparing nurse practitioners who are not only skilled clinicians but also compassionate, thoughtful professionals who make a difference in their communities. I couldn’t be more proud of what we’ve achieved together.”

Graceland’snurse practitioner programis known for its rigorous academic standards and personalized support, helping students excel in their careers. With a combination of innovative curriculum and real-world clinical experiences, the program equips graduates to meet the evolving demands of the healthcare industry. Graceland offers master’s of nursing in three online options: family nurse practitioner, acute care adult gerontology nurse practitioner, and psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner. Students can also gain certificates in those same disciplines and a post-graduate educator certificate.View all Graceland’s nursing programs.

This recognition reaffirms Graceland’s position as a leader in nursing education and strengthens the university’s mission to empower students for meaningful lives and productive careers.
