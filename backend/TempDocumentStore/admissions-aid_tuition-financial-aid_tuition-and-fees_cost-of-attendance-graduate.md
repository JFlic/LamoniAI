The cost of attendance is an estimate of the costs you can expect to pay for one year at Graceland University.These costs will be set up as your financial aid budget and used to determine the maximum amount of financial aid needed. You can use these amounts to help you create your own budget to pay for your education.

The cost of attendance is broken down into charges you will pay to the University and other estimated expenses you may have based on your spending habits.
